# Product Status

OpenSoundboard is an offline-first PySide6 desktop soundboard prototype.

## Delivered

- SQLite-backed boards and sounds with a default **My Sounds** board
- Import of WAV, MP3, OGG, FLAC, and M4A files by managed copy or source reference
- Normalized-path duplicate protection, missing-file detection, and partial-import summaries
- Qt Multimedia local playback, master volume, Stop All, and overlap/stop-previous selection
- PySide6 board sidebar, sound grid, import dialog, drag-and-drop import, and status bar

## Deferred

Global hotkeys, backups, output-device selection, search, sorting, list view, reorder, packaging,
and advanced settings are not implemented in this prototype.
